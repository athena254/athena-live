# Freelance/Hiring Platform Research

Researching platforms with APIs for freelancer hiring. Last updated: 2026-02-27.

---

## Upwork

### Public API?
**Yes** - Upwork has a public API (v3). The API is available at `https://www.upwork.com/ab/api/v3/`. It requires authentication and is designed for:
- Freelancer profile management
- Job searching and proposals
- Time tracking and billing
- Workroom management

### How Do Freelancers Get Hired?
1. Create a profile highlighting skills, experience, and hourly rate
2. Browse and apply to jobs (Connects system - freelancers purchase "Connects" to bid on jobs)
3. Clients review proposals and invite freelancers to interview
4. Hire based on interview and work history

### Application Process
- Open to anyone to sign up
- Identity verification required
- No formal vetting - reputation built through reviews and ratings
- Profile strength matters for visibility

## Toptal

### Public API?
**No** - Toptal does NOT have a public API. It's a curated network with manual matching.

### How Do Freelancers Get Hired?
1. Client discusses needs with a Toptal client advisor
2. Toptal hand-selects talent within 24 hours (typically)
3. Client works with matched developer for a trial period
4. Only pay if satisfied

### Application Process (For Freelancers)
- Extremely selective (top 3% of talent)
- Rigorous vetting: 5+ years industry experience required
- English proficiency expected
- Technical interviews and practical assessments
- No open registration - application by invitation or request

## Arc.dev

### Public API?
**No** - Arc does NOT have a public API. It's a managed talent matching platform.

### How Do Freelancers Get Hired?
1. Create profile with basic details and work experience
2. Complete vetting process (communication test + domain expertise interview)
3. Get recommended directly to hiring managers
4. Interview and receive offers

### Application Process
- Sign up free (no cost to join or use)
- Two-step vetting:
  - **Communication test**: Self-introduction, background questions
  - **Domain expertise interview**: Technical test for freelance roles
- Best candidates: 5+ years experience, excellent English
- 4.2K+ matches made, $45M+ earned through platform

---

## Gun.io

### Public API?
**No** - Gun.io does NOT have a public API. It's a managed high-stakes engineering platform.

### How Do Freelancers Get Hired?
1. Gun.io handles sourcing, vetting, and delivery management
2. Platform produces "high-precision shortlist" of vetted developers
3. Client is matched to engineers based on stack and delivery goals
4. Gun.io manages contracts, payroll, compliance

### Application Process
- Gun.io claims to have thousands of vetted engineers
- Focus on "Technical Review Protocol" - structured assessment before engagement
- Engineers evaluated against their standard with verified delivery history
- Global operations across 100+ countries with payroll/compliance handled
