> *From Greek 픣풠풭픡: "end, purpose, goal"*

## What is TELOS?

TELOS is a structured methodology for managing your personal objectives. It provides:
- Clarity on what matters most
- A framework for prioritization
- A way to track progress across all life domains
- AI-assisted goal management through Athena
