# Strategies

## Active Strategies

### Strategy 1: Build in Public
**Purpose**: Accelerate learning, build reputation, attract opportunities

**Approach**:
- Document progress publicly (Twitter, blog, GitHub)
- Share failures as well as successes
- Engage with community feedback

**Tactics**:
- [ ] Weekly progress updates on social media
- [ ] Open source non-proprietary components
- [ ] Write detailed technical blog posts
- [ ] Engage in relevant communities (Discord, Reddit, HN)

**Success Indicators**:
- Growing follower count
- Meaningful engagement on posts
- Incoming opportunities from visibility

**Related Goals**: Open Source Contribution, Financial Independence

**Purpose**: Reduce risk, increase stability

**Approach**:

- Reinvest in growth

**Tactics**:
- [ ] Freelance income (Upwork, direct clients)
- [ ] Product income (SaaS, digital products)
- [ ] Investment income (dividends, interest)
- [ ] Consulting income (expertise monetization)

**Success Indicators**:
- Revenue from 3+ sources

- Growing passive income percentage

**Related Goals**: Financial Independence
