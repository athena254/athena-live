Privacy Policy

Athena Live

Your Privacy Matters

At Athena Live, we believe your privacy is fundamental. This policy explains what data we collect, why we collect it, and how we protect it. We're committed to transparency and giving you control over your information.

Information We Collect

We collect minimal data necessary to provide our services:

  • Account Information: Email address, username, and password (encrypted)
  • Usage Data: How you interact with our service to improve functionality
  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, and IP address for security
  • Communication: Messages sent through our platform (end-to-end encrypted)

How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to:

  • Provide and maintain our services
  • Improve user experience and functionality
  • Ensure security and prevent abuse
  • Communicate important updates and announcements
  • Comply with legal obligations
We do NOT:
  • Sell your personal data to third parties
  • Use your data for advertising without consent
  • Share your communications with anyone
  • Track you across other websites

Data Protection

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • End-to-end encryption for all communications
  • Secure data storage with encryption at rest
  • Regular security audits and updates
  • Limited employee access to user data
  • Two-factor authentication support

Your Rights

You have full control over your data:

  • Access: Request a copy of your data
  • Correction: Update or correct your information
  • Deletion: Request complete account deletion
  • Export: Download your data in a portable format
  • Opt-out: Disable non-essential data collection

Third-Party Services

We may use trusted third-party services for specific functions (payment processing, analytics). These partners are bound by strict confidentiality agreements and cannot use your data for their own purposes.

Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy periodically. We'll notify you of significant changes via email or in-app notification. Continued use of our service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
